Newark Fulfillment Center

Newark Fulfillment Center is a series of warehouses that services local small businesses who do not possess suitable space to hold/hwill send ost their inventory.

Just like AFS, small business inventory directly to NFC to store, process and fulfill orders from health and well being to medical supplies such as PPE all the way to dog/pet grooming and clothing.

NFC is a self-contained system that no outsiders are allowed into the facilities, and everything needs to be checked-in and checked-out through ownership account.
